About

Three decades at the frontier of legal leadership

Bjarne P. Tellmann is a recognised legal and business leader, speaker, and author whose work focuses on the future of legal services, technology-enabled transformation, and leadership in the legal profession.

As Founding General Counsel and Executive Committee member at Haleon (FTSE 20), he co-led the legal and compliance work for the company's historic de-merger from GSK. As CLO and GC at Pearson (FTSE 100), he redesigned and digitised the legal department — reducing costs by more than 40% while improving service quality.

He has held senior roles at Coca-Cola, Aramco, and Kimberly-Clark, and practised at Sullivan & Cromwell LLP and White & Case LLP across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and the U.S.

Today, Bjarne is CEO of FjordStream Advisors GmbH, a boutique consultancy working with legal leaders and law firms on strategy, transformation, and leadership. He is also a Senior Visiting Fellow at the London School of Economics.

Services

Strategic Advisory

Bjarne regularly advises senior executives and law firm leaders globally to help them transform their organisations and optimise service delivery models. Projects include advising on how to leverage digital innovation and integrate cutting-edge technology; attract, develop and retain top talent; shape high-performing cultures that thrive in any environment; and lead teams with clarity and confidence through disruption and change.

Services

Executive Coaching

Bjarne is a Henley Business School trained and EMCC (European Mentoring and Coaching Council) Certified Coach. He creates an environment that gives executives the space to discover solutions, unlock insights, define their purpose, align their careers with their values, and navigate professional and life transitions.

Many of his clients are senior leaders undergoing various professional transitions, including promotions, new assignments, career moves, or life changes. He brings decades of global corporate executive experience to his coaching and has personally experienced many of the challenges that ambitious, high-performing professionals encounter in navigating their lives and careers. While this enables him to empathise, he firmly believes every person's situation — and the challenges and goals they face — are unique to them.
